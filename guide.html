Enemy Mobs

Learn about the hostile creatures you'll encounter in Minecraft, and prepare to defend yourself!

Zombie

A common enemy that spawns at night. Zombies can swarm you if you're not careful!

Skeleton

Skeletons attack from a distance with their bows. Avoid their arrows to stay alive.

Creeper

Explodes when near you. Be cautious around these silent but deadly mobs.

Bogged

Variant of a skeleton found in swamp biomes. It is an undead hostile mob that can shoot poisoned arrows.

Stray

Variant of a skeleton that spawns in snow-covered biomes. Fires tipped arrows of Slowness from their bows.

Phantom

Flying undead hostile mobs that spawn when the player hasn't slept for 3 or more in-game days.

Zombie Villager

Variant of zombie that can be cured into a villager using a golden apple while it's under the effect of Weakness.

Husk

Variant of a zombie that spawns in deserts. They do not burn in sunlight and inflict Hunger with their unarmed attacks.

Slime

Bouncy, cube-shaped hostile mobs that spawn deep underground in particular chunks, or in swamp biomes.

Witch

Uses potions in combat, both in the form of weapons as splash potions and in the form of drinkable potions for defense.

Silverfish

Infest stone and deepslate blocks in mountains, strongholds, igloo basements and woodland mansions.

Endermite

An endermite may spawn when a player throws an ender pearl.

Ghast

Large white floating jellyfish ghosts that live in the Nether and shoot explosive fireballs at the player.

Wither Skeleton

Tall black variants of skeletons equipped with stone swords that inflict the poison-like Wither effect.

Magma Cube

Behaves similarly to a slime, but is fireproof, jumps higher and less often, and deals more damage

Blaze

Spawns in nether fortresses and attacks at range by firing fireballs or with their spinning rods.

Hoglin

Attacks by thrusting its tusks upward, which can also launch its target a short distance into the air.

Piglin Brute

Hostile and stronger variant of piglins that appears in all types of bastion remnants.

Zoglin

Undead hostile mob created when a hoglin enters the Overworld or End.

Guardian

Spawns in and around ocean monuments. They attack with laser beam, and inflict retaliatory damage when damaged by a melee attack.

Elder Guardian

Stronger and larger variant of a guardian and attacks like a guardian, but also applies Mining Fatigue.

Pillager

Armed with crossbows found in wandering patrols, in pillager outposts, or as participants in raids.

Evoker

Spell-casting illager found in woodland mansions and raids, and the only source of the totem of undying.

Vex

It wields an iron sword, can phase through walls, and is only summoned as one of the evoker's attacks.

Vindicator

Illager equipped with an iron axe. Vindicators appear in woodland mansions and participate in raids.

Breeze

Floating hostile mob that is similar to the blaze that spawns in trial chambers. It attacks at range by firing wind charges.

Shulker

Found in end cities, It hides in its shell to protect itself and its attacks inflict Levitation.

Wither

Player-constructed and highly destructive boss mob that can hover in mid-air and fire explosive at its target.

Warden

Extremely powerful mob boss summoned by sculk shriekers in the deep dark biome.

Ender Dragon

Giant flying boss mob found when first entering the End. It guards the portal that allows return to the Overworld.

Starting Crafts

Learn about the basic tools that you have to make to survive.

Wooden Planks

The raw wood you got from a tree can be crafted into more planks which can be used for tools.

Crafting Table

Crafting table makes is for making all the tools and other materials you're gonna need.

Wooden Axe

Use this to chop down trees more effectively and faster rather than using your hands.

Wooden Pickaxe

Use this to mine stones and minerals more effectively and faster rather than using your hands.

Wooden Sword

Use this to gather food and kill mobs more effectively and faster rather than using your hands.

Wooden Shovel

Use this to collect kinds of block effectively and faster rather than using your hands.

Chest

Use this to store tools and materials more effectively rather than using your small inventory.

Wooden Door

Use this to provide door to your shelter to protect you from mobs and even breath underwater.

Furnace

Use this to cook your food to replenish your hunger bar.
